About me

I'm a visual designer who works across the full digital campaign stack — from illustrated email banners to motion-designed video ads.

My background is in-house at SaaS and loyalty platforms, working with travel and hospitality clients including JetBlue, United Airlines, and Choice Hotels. I'm looking to bring that multi-channel production experience into an agency environment.

I studied Interactive Media Design at Durham College — a technical foundation that bleeds into my design approach. I think in formats, contexts, and constraints, not just aesthetics.
