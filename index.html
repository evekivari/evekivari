JetBlue Airlines

Rebrand campaign — Email · Display · Storefront

JetBlue had just rebranded and needed a full campaign suite that lived up to their updated visual identity. I audited their existing email creative and brand guidelines to identify their visual language — flat 2D illustration — then explored two directions: a minimal single-illustration approach and a fully illustrated aspirational background. The client chose the aspirational route. That illustrated environment became the consistent thread across all formats.

Video · Motion

United Airlines

CTV & YouTube Ad — After Effects · Premiere Pro

I proposed video advertising as a new creative channel for the agency — and when United Airlines agreed to pilot it, I owned the project end-to-end. Working from United's motion toolkit and brand guidelines, I produced a 30-second ad in Premiere Pro and After Effects, built for both YouTube and CTV formats. Logo animation was synced to the track's beat; a slow zoom on the hero image created motion from a static asset. Copy pacing was calibrated for on-screen read time across large-format TV viewing.

About me

I'm a visual designer who works across the full digital campaign stack — from illustrated email banners to motion-designed video ads. I'm comfortable owning a brief end to end, and equally comfortable slotting into a larger creative team.

My background is in-house at SaaS and loyalty platforms, working with travel and hospitality clients including JetBlue, United Airlines, and Choice Hotels. I'm now looking to bring that multi-channel production experience into an agency environment.

I studied Interactive Media Design at Durham College, which gave me a technical foundation that bleeds into my design approach — I think in formats, contexts, and constraints, not just aesthetics.
