Multi-channel campaign

JetBlue Airlines

Scope

Email · Display · Storefront

Role

Visual Designer

Deliverables

6 format types across campaign

JetBlue had just rebranded and needed a full campaign suite that reflected their updated visual identity.

I started by auditing their existing email creative and studying their brand guidelines to identify the visual language they were developing — landing on their use of simple, flat 2D illustration as the direction. I explored two routes: a minimal approach using a single small illustration tied to the copy, and a more aspirational direction with a fully illustrated background environment. The client selected the aspirational route. That illustrated mountain landscape became the consistent visual thread across all campaign formats.
